First principles

Three rules we built Roam around.

01

Act inside the workflow.

30+ steps per file across SoftPro, NextAce, inHere. AI that suggests buys minutes. AI that acts inside the system of record compounds across the file.

Assist wins minutes. Act wins files.
02

Your rules. Not a black box.

Your offices, your states, your underwriter mix, your office-level norms. California is community property. Florida charges doc stamps. Your Tennessee searches differ from your Georgia ones. Your rules live as data your team can read, edit, and audit — not buried inside one giant AI prompt nobody controls.

Editable by your ops lead. Not a model upgrade away.
03

Humans own the consequential calls.

ALTA BP defines the chain of custody. Agent takes the first pass. Licensed humans own every decision that can cost a claim, a policy, or a license.

A human should never take the first pass again.
Security & control

Three things Roam never touches: your money · your data · your underwriter relationships.

Your money

Wires stay with your licensed team.

Disbursement, wires, and high-risk escrow are always handled by your people — never by AI. CertifID and FundingShield verify every wire instruction and callback. BEC (business email compromise) attempts are flagged and blocked. ALTA Best Practices 4.0 built in, not bolted on.

Your data

Files stay behind your firewall.

Roam runs inside your environment. Your files aren't copied to Roam's cloud. Roam doesn't train on your data. Every read is logged and auditable. SOC 2 Type II audit in progress. ISO 27001 + ISO 42001 in progress.

Your underwriter

Agents prepare. Your licensed team signs.

Underwriting approval, commitment issuance, and disbursement always stay with your team. You set approval thresholds office by office. Every agent action is logged and reversible. Your relationship with First American, FNF, Stewart, or Old Republic never sees Roam.
